Hi! Thanks for visiting our page. My name is Eve, and I’m the creator of Creating As We Grow. A bit about me:
I’m a mum of three with a background in data analysis. I’m also an aspiring software developer and tester, with a Level 4 BCS certification in full‑stack web development — and yes, I built this website myself.
Now onto the brand:
I started this account during lockdown while on maternity leave. With children’s clubs closed, I began creating simple activities at home to keep my little ones engaged. At the time they were between 0 and 2 years old, so the play naturally ranged from sensory exploration to early problem‑solving. I quickly realised how much I enjoyed designing these moments for them, and I wanted to keep nurturing that creativity as they grew, because learning never really stops. That’s where the name Creating As We Grow comes from — a reminder that creativity evolves alongside us. Sharing these experiences with my children and watching their curiosity unfold has been one of the most rewarding parts of this journey.
